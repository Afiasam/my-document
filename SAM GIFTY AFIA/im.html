Small History About kente

The invention of kente cloth is often attributed to the Ashanti people in Ghana. An Ashanti legend called The Spider Weaver tells how two young men were taught to weave kente cloth by a Spider.You can listen to the story any time you visit this site.

The orgins of Kente cloth date back to 12th century Africa,in the country of Ghana and the Ashanti people. The cloth was worn by Kings,Queens,and important figures of state in Ghana's society during ceremonial events and special occasions.The colors of Kente cloth have symbolic meanings which inspire others to use it still now
